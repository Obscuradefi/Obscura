The Stablecoin Commerce Stack Challenge is a 3-month virtual program led by Ignyte, with Circle and Arc as technical sponsors, bringing together developers to explore how stablecoins can address key regional opportunities across:

Cross-border payments and remittances
SME finance and trade workflows
Tokenized assets and compliant DeFi
The agentic economy
For educational and testnet demo purposes only

Why Build on Arc?
Arc is a purpose-built L1 blockchain from Circle, designed to support programmable, stablecoin-based applications. It provides the foundation for developers to build and test financial systems with predictable costs, real-time settlement, and programmable logic.

With Arc, developers can:

Settle transactions in USDC with predictable, dollar-denominated fees
Achieve deterministic finality for real-time financial workflows
Build programmable payment flows using smart contracts
Support cross-chain USDC movement with Circle infrastructure
Design systems for payments, distribution, and financial workflows

Tracks
1) Best Cross-Border Payments & Remittances Experience (UAE → Global)
Prize:

1st Place - 5000 USDC
2nd Place - 3000 USDC
Goal: Build instant, low-cost cross-border payment and remittance flows using Circle’s regulated stablecoins—optimized for UAE’s high-expat corridor needs (consumer remittances, B2B settlement, global payroll, freelancer payouts).

What to build (examples):

Remittance app with transparent fees + real-time settlement confirmation
Marketplace settlement (UAE-based platform paying global sellers/creators)
Global payroll / contractor payouts with stablecoin settlement and receipts
“Pay-in AED, settle in USDC” merchant settlement prototype (conceptual UX + rails)
Recommended Circle tools on Arc:

USDC (primary stablecoin rail)
CCTP with Bridge Kit (cross-chain USDC transfers where relevant)
Circle Wallets (embedded wallet UX for non-crypto-native users)
Circle Gateway (routing, treasury movement, settlement workflows)
Gated / advanced tools:

StableFX - for FX-aware settlement concepts / multi-currency routing

Stablecoin escrow for import/export settlement (milestone-based release)

3) Best Real World Asset Tokenization on Arc with Embedded Compliance

1st Place - 3000 USDC

This track focuses on how stablecoins and smart contracts can power the next generation of regulated digital assets in the UAE and broader GCC, where tokenization, fixed income innovation, and compliant capital markets infrastructure are rapidly evolving.

4) Best Agentic Economy Experience on Arc
Prize:

1st Place - 4000 USDC
2nd Place - 2000 USDC
Goal:
Build autonomous economic experiences where AI agents can research, negotiate, and execute transactions on behalf of users or businesses using onchain rails and programmable payment logic.

What to build (examples):

An AI agent that autonomously discovers and executes a stablecoin-settled purchase using Arc smart contracts
Automated subscription purchase workflows where agents manage renewal, budgeting, and payment authorization
Autonomous merchant settlement systems where AI negotiates and settles with multiple onchain counterparties
AI-driven loyalty programs that reason about cost, rewards, and payment routing across wallets and chains

Nanopayments - enable high-frequency, sub-cent transactions for real-time agentic payment flows
